🚨 SAR/STR Explained for Kids! 🚨

What is SAR or STR?

SAR = Suspicious Activity Report

STR = Suspicious Transaction Report

These are like "ALERT!" messages when something fishy 🐟 happens with money.

🤔 What Does Suspicious Mean?

Something that feels weird or wrong 👣

🏦 Lemonade Stand Story

😊 Normal Kid

  • Saves $10 regularly
  • Everything looks normal ✅

😕 Suspicious Kid

  • Suddenly brings $500 🚨
  • Secretive and nervous 👀
  • Comes at night 🌙

Bank Manager: "This is weird! Let me report it!" 📋

🚗 Car Wash Example

  • Same person comes daily
  • Pays $99 repeatedly
  • Never collects car

🚨 Red Flag: Breaking big money into small pieces

🔍 Red Flags

💰 Money Patterns

  • Structuring (splitting money)
  • Round-tripping (in & out quickly)
  • Sudden large deposits
  • Odd timings

👤 Customer Behavior

  • Unknown source of money
  • Different behavior
  • Hidden identity 🕵️

⚖️ Who Does What?

  • 🏦 Bank → Detects & reports
  • 🚔 Police → Investigates
  • ⚖️ Lawyers → Ensure rules

🌍 Countries Rules

  • 🇬🇧 UK → SAR → 7 days
  • 🇪🇺 Europe → STR → 2-10 days
  • 🇺🇸 USA → SAR → 30 days

🎮 Examples

🍕 Pizza Shop

🎮 Game Store

🎯 Super Simple Flow

💰 Bank sees weird activity ↓ 🚨 Red flag ↓ 📋 Report ↓ 🚔 Police investigate ↓ ⚖️ Action
